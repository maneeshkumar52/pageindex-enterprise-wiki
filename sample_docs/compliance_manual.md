# Compliance Manual — Acme Corporation

## 1. Purpose and Scope

This Compliance Manual establishes the framework for ensuring Acme Corporation adheres to all applicable laws, regulations, and internal standards. It applies to all employees, contractors, consultants, and third-party partners.

## 2. Regulatory Framework

### 2.1 Data Protection
Acme Corporation complies with the following data protection regulations:
- **GDPR** (General Data Protection Regulation) — for EU operations
- **CCPA** (California Consumer Privacy Act) — for US operations
- **PIPEDA** (Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act) — for Canadian operations

### 2.2 Financial Regulations
- SOX (Sarbanes-Oxley Act) compliance for financial reporting
- Anti-Money Laundering (AML) requirements
- Know Your Customer (KYC) procedures for client onboarding

### 2.3 Industry-Specific Standards
- ISO 27001 — Information Security Management
- SOC 2 Type II — Service Organization Controls
- PCI DSS — for payment card processing

## 3. Data Handling Procedures

### 3.1 Data Classification

| Level | Description | Examples | Handling |
|-------|------------|----------|----------|
| **Public** | Non-sensitive, publicly available | Marketing materials, press releases | No restrictions |
| **Internal** | For internal use only | Meeting notes, internal memos | Do not share externally |
| **Confidential** | Sensitive business data | Financial reports, strategic plans | Encrypt at rest and in transit |
| **Restricted** | Highly sensitive | PII, health records, trade secrets | Encrypt, log access, need-to-know basis |

### 3.2 Data Retention
- Financial records: 7 years
- Employee records: Duration of employment + 5 years
- Customer data: Duration of relationship + 3 years
- Audit logs: 5 years
- Marketing data: Until consent withdrawal + 30 days

### 3.3 Data Disposal
- Digital data must be securely wiped using NIST 800-88 compliant methods.
- Physical documents must be shredded using cross-cut shredders.
- Disposal must be logged in the Data Disposal Register.

## 4. Anti-Bribery and Corruption

### 4.1 Policy Statement
Acme Corporation prohibits all forms of bribery and corruption. This includes:
- Offering, giving, or receiving bribes
- Facilitation payments
- Kickbacks or undisclosed commissions
- Gifts exceeding USD 100 in value to government officials

### 4.2 Gift and Entertainment Policy
- Gifts over USD 50 must be reported to the Compliance Officer.
- Entertainment expenses exceeding USD 200 require prior approval.
- All gifts to/from government officials must be pre-approved.

## 5. Whistleblower Protection

### 5.1 Reporting Channels
Employees can report compliance concerns through:
1. **Direct reporting** to the Compliance Officer
2. **Ethics Hotline:** 1-800-ACME-ETHICS (available 24/7)
3. **Online portal:** compliance.acme-corp.example.com
4. **Written report:** Compliance Office, Floor 12, HQ Building

### 5.2 Protection Guarantees
- Reports can be made anonymously
- No retaliation against good-faith reporters
- Independent investigation within 30 days
- Quarterly reporting to the Board Audit Committee

## 6. Training Requirements

### 6.1 Mandatory Training
| Course | Frequency | Audience |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| Anti-Bribery & Corruption | Annual | All employees |
| Data Protection (GDPR/CCPA) | Annual | All employees |
| Information Security | Annual | All employees |
| Insider Trading Prevention | Annual | Finance, Legal, Executives |
| AML/KYC | Annual | Client-facing roles |

### 6.2 Compliance Certification
All employees must complete annual compliance certification by December 31. Failure to complete certification may result in suspension of system access.

## 7. Incident Response

### 7.1 Data Breach Response
1. **Identify** — Detect and classify the breach within 1 hour.
2. **Contain** — Isolate affected systems immediately.
3. **Notify** — Alert the CISO and DPO within 4 hours.
4. **Investigate** — Complete root cause analysis within 72 hours.
5. **Report** — Notify relevant authorities within 72 hours (GDPR requirement).
6. **Remediate** — Implement fixes and update security controls.
7. **Review** — Post-incident review within 2 weeks.

### 7.2 Escalation Matrix
| Severity | Response Time | Escalation To |
|----------|--------------|---------------|
| Critical | 15 minutes | CISO, CEO, Legal |
| High | 1 hour | CISO, VP Engineering |
| Medium | 4 hours | Security Team Lead |
| Low | 24 hours | IT Security Analyst |

## 8. Audit and Monitoring

### 8.1 Internal Audits
- Quarterly compliance audits by the Internal Audit team
- Annual external audit by independent auditors
- Continuous monitoring of access logs and transactions
- Random sampling of data handling practices

### 8.2 Key Metrics
- Compliance training completion rate (target: 100%)
- Incident response time vs. SLA
- Open compliance findings and remediation progress
- Whistleblower report resolution time
